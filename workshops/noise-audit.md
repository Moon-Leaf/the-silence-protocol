# Workshop: Noise Audit

**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Goal:** Map your team's digital noise sources and decide on the first steps to reduce them.

## Preparation (before the session)
- Each member tracks all notifications received during one workday (type, app, time).
- Prepare a physical whiteboard or virtual canvas (Miro, Mural).

## Workshop Flow

### 0–5 min: Opening
Explain the goal: “We're going to identify what disturbs us most and agree on how to reduce it.”

### 5–20 min: Disturbance Mapping
- Each member writes down every notification source on sticky notes.
- Group them: *Urgent & Important*, *Important but Not Urgent*, *Not Important*.
- Discuss: which ones most harm focus?

### 20–35 min: Calculate the Cost
- How many interruptions per hour on average?
- Multiply by the average time to regain focus (~23 minutes).
- Estimate total productive hours lost per week.

### 35–50 min: Design Solutions
- Pick a maximum of 2 changes to implement over the next 2 weeks.
- Example: “All non-urgent notifications are silenced between 09:00–12:00.”
- Write them into your team contract (see [Communication Contract](../templates/communication-contract.md)).

### 50–60 min: Commitment
- Each member voices agreement.
- Set a follow-up date.

## Follow-Up
- Use the upcoming quarterly review template (in `workshops/`) to evaluate.
- Repeat the audit every 3–6 months.
