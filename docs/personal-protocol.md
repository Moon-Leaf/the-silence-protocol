# Personal Protocol – A Guide for Individuals

You don't need a team to start. You can begin *The Silence Protocol* today, on your own.

## Core Principles
1. **You own your attention.** Not apps, not notifications.
2. **Start small.** One small, consistent change beats a failed revolution.
3. **Let people know.** The protocol works best when others understand your rules.

## Three Levels of Adoption

### 🌱 Seed (Beginner)
- Disable all notifications except calls.
- Establish one screen-free morning (e.g., 06:00–08:00).
- Set a simple auto-reply: *“I check email at 09:00 and 15:00.”*

### 🌿 Sprout (Intermediate)
- Schedule a 90-minute *Focus Block* every workday.
- Use `deep_focus` mode manually.
- Share your `Response Window` with close contacts.

### 🌳 Root (Advanced)
- Design your day around energy, not just hours.
- Use a *Reachability Profile* to differentiate who can break through silence modes.
- Reflect weekly using the [journal template](../journal/template.md).
