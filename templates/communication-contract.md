## 2. Communication Channels

## 3. Focus Modes
Every member has the right to activate `deep_focus` or `do_not_disturb`.  
While active:
- Messages will not interrupt.
- Only emergency calls with the passphrase will break through.

## 4. After-Hours Communication
- No obligation to reply outside work hours.
- Use message scheduling to send at the recipient's next Response Window.
